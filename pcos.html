PCOS

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome

Do's And Dont's

1) Make sure that proper weight is maintained. If you are overweight, reduce it by doing exercises regularly and having a healthy balanced diet.
2) Practice Yoga Asanas and Prananyam regularly.
3) Have adequate sleep.
4) Maintain a record of your menstrual cycle.
5) Do not smoke and consume alcohol.
6) Do not skip meals or sleep. A disciplined lifestyle is key to better health and freedom from PCOS.
7) Avoid Hormonal treatment as far as possible.

Food Items You Can Easily Consume

1) High-fiber foods: , cauliflower, red and green peppers, almonds, sweet potatoes, and pumpkin
2) Lean proteins: , fish, and egg whites
3) Anti-inflammatory foods:, spinach, strawberries, walnuts, almonds, turmeric, and fatty fish such as sardines and salmon
